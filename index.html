General Roleplay Rules

  1. Please use common sense when applicable
  2. Do not randomly kill people (Random Deathmatch | RDM)
  3. Do not scam players
  4. Do not Prop Minge (Climb | Block | Kill | Trap | Push)
  5. Do not set fires in unreachable places
  6. Do not bait (Lure people into death)
  7. Do not metagame
  8. Hitbox exploiting is not permitted
  9. Do not go afk as any job just to earn money
  10. Do not job abuse (buying guns and then switching)

New Life Rule (NLR)

  1. When you die you do not remember anything from your previous life
  2. You may not return to the area you died in for 5 minutes

General Raid Rules

  1. You must use the command “/advert raid” before raiding
  2. Do not re-lock your doors whilst being raided

Raiding Requirements

  1. Professional & Amatuer thief may solo raid
  2. Rebels must raid in groups of 2+
  3. Gangsters must raid with their mob boss
  4. If the mob boss dies, the raid is over
  5. Hitmen can raid to kill target
  6. However you must only kill their target unless fired upon first
  7. Terrorists must in groups of 2+

Building

  1. Buildings must be structurally sound and realistic (EG: No SkyBases)
  2. You may only have 2 fading doors between you and your base
  3. You must have 2 keypads per fading door (Way in, way out)
  4. No hitbox exploiting
  5. No material glitching (1 way props to shoot through)
  6. Areas used to shoot through must be at least a keypad tall
  7. No crouch Entrances
Additional information

Since it's difficult to list every possible rule, common sense will be heavily enfored. If you don't think you should do it, don't.
